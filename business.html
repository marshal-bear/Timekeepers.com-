Industry sector
expertise

Express transport to meet your challenges.

From the diagnosis of your needs to the management of the service, through offers and a dedicated customer service, we provide an appropriate response to your challenges, the constraints of your business and the expectations of your customers.

Our solutions in metropolitan France

Reduce stocks and those of your customers while remaining responsive, major logistical challenges for the satisfaction of your customers.

Optimize your logistics to the maximum to meet the "0 stock" management challenges of your customers

  • Deliveries of express industrial products the next day before 1pm throughout France to avoid stock-outs and quickly replenish your recipients.
  • Delivery services before 8am, before 9am or before 10am* for your most urgent flows.
  • The daily collection of all your sites may include several passages as well as late removals.
  • The transport of non-standard or dangerous products thanks to a precise audit.
  • Express management of the after-sales service (returns of defective goods and exchanges on site) to provide an optimal service to your customers.

Offer value-added services for your itinerant business recipients

  • A wide choice of deposit and withdrawal points: 10,000 Post Offices, 15,000 Relais Pickup and 81 Chronopost Agencies.
  • Information by email or SMS of the availability of the package at a point of proximity.

Allow you to gain productivity

  • An after-sales unit specialized in the different trades of the industry.
  • Monthly reports to better manage your business.
  • Satisfaction studies with your customers to evaluate our service.
  • The tracking of your shipments in real time with the channel of your choice (web, email, SMS)

* Benefit available
on study ** Benefit available for 25,000 municipalities in France

 

Our international solutions

For your international development we have all the device that will allow you to reach a wider clientele, in Europe, but also in the rest of the world. Conquer new markets with express delays ranging from 24 to 48 hours *** to 230 countries and territories.

  • Chronopost, number 1 in France for express delivery
  • A single carrier, for your domestic and international shipments.
  • Delivery solutions adapted with different degrees of speed depending on the destination concerned to shorten the time between taking the order and delivering the package to your foreign customer.


    •  

Chronopost is a customs agent and authorized economic operator, thus facilitating the customs clearance and export of your goods. And to allow you to develop even more in the overseas territories, we take care of your heavy and large shipments to the DOM with our Dom Freight solution.

The deadlines mentioned are the best time expressed in working days. They are indicative and are subject to change. They agree to take charge from Monday to Friday, except for shipments delivered on Saturday for which the deadlines run from the following Monday.
