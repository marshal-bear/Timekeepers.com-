How do I send a parcel by express?

With Chronopost, sending a parcel by express is done with ease. We offer you the possibility to make your express shipments of goods and folds directly online. Create your account for free and send your package to France and abroad. Simply fill in the places of departure and destination to view the different Chronopost offers. You can also take advantage of the Ready-to-Ship available online,at the post office or at the Chronopost branch. Our 7 packaging sizes are designed to fit all your shipments of goods.

Estimate the cost of your shipment and know the delivery times

By choosing to send your express package online, you can simulate the cost of your shipment. The delivery time is also indicated according to the chosen destination. With Chronopost offers, the delivery of your goods remains fast and secure. Take advantage of an advantageous rate and send your parcels by express from 11,10 € HT4.

Properly prepare the express shipment of a package

So that the deliveries of your express parcel shipments take place in optimal conditions, Chronopost gives you its advice to properly pack your goods (weight, dimensions, adapted packaging, etc.). The express transport of parcels internationally requires compliance with customs formalities specific to each destination. For this, we invite you to consult the sheets of the countries served by Chronopost.
