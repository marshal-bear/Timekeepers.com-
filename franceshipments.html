How do I send a parcel by express?

With Chronopost, sending a parcel by express is done with ease. We offer you the possibility to carry out your shipment of goods directly online. Create your account for free and send your package to France and abroad at the best price. All you have to do is fill in the places of departure and destination to display the various Chronopost offers for parcel delivery by hand, in a box or letter or in a relay. You can also take advantage of the Ready-to-Ship available on laposte.fr,post office or Chronopost agency to send parcels. Our 7 packaging sizes are designed to fit all your shipments of goods.

Estimate the cost of your shipment and know the delivery times

By choosing to send your express package online, you can simulate the cost of your shipment. The delivery time is also indicated according to the chosen destination in working days. With Chronopost offers, the delivery of your goods remains fast and secure from the departure of your package to its arrival.

Properly prepare the express shipment of a package

So that your express parcel shipment takes place in optimal conditions, Chronopost gives you its advice to properly pack your goods (weight, dimensions, adapted packaging, etc.). Sending a package internationally requires compliance with customs formalities specific to each destination in order to simplify its routing. For this, we invite you to consult the country sheets served by Chronopost to send a package.

Send a package

At home, at the office or at the holiday resort, your package is delivered the next morning to the address of your choice! And you make your transport label online with ease.

Your possibilities:

  • Delivery the next day before 1pm anywhere in metropolitan France.
  • Delivery the next day before 10am, to more than 25,000 municipalities.

Send a package in relay

Your package is dropped off the next day before 1 p.m. in the pickup relay of your choice depending on the address of your recipient. This solution is available by making your transport label online for a quick send.

Your package is delivered the next morning before 1pm in one of the 15,000 pickup relays of your choice. These relays (a bookstore, a florist, a supermarket, a gas station ...) are selected for their hourly ampitude (open late at night, on weekends) and have a dedicated storage space.

And there is always a relay close to your recipient or his place of work: 95% of the French population is less than 15 minutes from a pickup relay.

To find the nearest pickup relay,enter the postal code.
